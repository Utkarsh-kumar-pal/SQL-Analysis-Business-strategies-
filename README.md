### 1. Executive Summary

This report synthesizes our recent sales data to reveal critical customer trends. The findings are clear: our market is not a single entity but a series of distinct segments defined by **Price**, **Popularity**, and **Product**.

The core of our market is the **3-to-4-bedroom house**. Our strategic "sweet spots" are two-fold: high-volume postcodes like **2615** and high-value postcodes like **2603** and **2600**. Seasonally, the market consistently peaks in **November**.

We must adopt a segmented strategy, aligning our portfolio, development, and marketing resources to capitalize on these specific, predictable trends.

### 3. Product Analysis: The "What" Customers Are Buying

#### üè° The Core Market: 3 & 4 Bedroom Homes
The data on bedroom trends (excluding units) is definitive. The vast majority of our market is purchasing 3 and 4-bedroom homes.

* **3 Bedrooms:** 11,281 sales (Avg. Price: $550,263)
* **4 Bedrooms:** 10,404 sales (Avg. Price: $718,450)
* **5 Bedrooms:** 1,947 sales (Avg. Price: $918,705)

**Economic Insight:** The market places a **$168,187 premium** on the 4th bedroom (the jump from 3-bed to 4-bed avg. price). This is a critical data point for pricing strategy and development planning.


#### ‚öñÔ∏è The Market Extremes
* **Luxury Ceiling (Top 10):** The high-end market (from $4.15M to $8.0M) consists **exclusively of 'houses'**, not units. These sales are concentrated in our premium postcodes (**2603, 2600**) and the diverse **2611**.
* **Entry-Level Floor (Lowest 10):** The entry-level market (from $56,500) is a mix of houses and units, spread across several postcodes including **2611, 2615, 2605,** and **2612**.

**The `2611` Paradox:** This postcode is a market of extremes, containing both the **#1 most expensive property ($8.0M)** and the **#1 least expensive property ($56,500)**. It cannot be treated as a single, uniform market.

### 5. Strategic Recommendations

1.  **Adopt a Two-Pronged Portfolio Strategy:**
    * **VOLUME:** Aggressively target **2615, 2602, and 2913** for market share, focusing on 3 and 4-bedroom properties.
    * **VALUE:** Target **2603 and 2600** for premium listings and high-margin developments, reinforcing our brand at the top end.

2.  **Focus Development on the "Plus-One" Premium:**
    * The **$168k premium for a 4th bedroom** is our most important development signal. Our new-build and renovation strategies should be heavily weighted toward 3 and 4-bedroom houses, as this is the clear core of customer demand.

3.  **Align All Operations for the Q4 Surge:**
    * Treat the November peak as the fiscal Super Bowl. All departments (Marketing, Sales, Operations) must have their resources, campaigns, and listings prepared and launched by October 1st.
